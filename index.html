Engagement models

Three ways to engage us. All partner-led.

We meet you where you are. Most roles run retained or engaged — but for the right opportunity, we'll work contingency too.

Most common

Retained Search

For C-suite, board-reporting, and mission-critical hires

30% of first-year cash compensation
  • One-third at engagement
  • One-third at ~day 45
  • One-third on placement
  • Exclusive, performance-milestone partnership
  • Up to 12-month replacement guarantee
  • Weekly partner-led updates

Engaged Search

For VP, Director, and senior manager hires

25% of first-year cash compensation
  • Engagement fee at start (credited to the fee)
  • Balance due on placement
  • Exclusive for 60–90 days
  • 6–12 month replacement guarantee
  • Bi-weekly updates with a partner
  • Full shortlist by day 21

Contingency

For active, time-sensitive roles where speed wins

25% of first-year base salary
  • No fee unless we place
  • 90-day exclusivity (no five firms chasing the same names)
  • 6-month replacement guarantee
  • Candidate slates as ready
  • Fast turnaround on active roles
  • Often the first step into a retained relationship

Private equity portfolio companies and multi-search relationships qualify for preferred terms. Ask us on the call.

The Turnkey Recruiting Guarantee

If the hire doesn't work out, we find the next one — at no cost.

Every placement is guaranteed — six months as standard, and up to twelve months on executive and retained searches. If your hire leaves or is terminated for cause inside the guarantee window, we restart the search at no additional fee. No mediation. No fine print. No asterisks. (A layoff or restructuring on your side doesn't count against you — the guarantee is about the fit, not your business decisions.)

Partner-led, every search.

You'll work directly with a partner — not a junior recruiter handed your search to learn on.

Transparent weekly updates.

You'll always know where the search stands, which candidates are progressing, and what the next decision is.

Off-limits, fully honored.

We do not recruit out of clients we've worked with. Standard 12-month off-limits — extended on retained relationships.

Our guarantee isn't marketing — it's how we filter the searches we take on. If we don't believe we can find the right person, we'll tell you on the first call.

Common questions

The questions we get most often.

What size companies do you typically work with? +

Most of our clients are mid-to-large companies — roughly $50M to $10B in revenue and 200 to 10,000+ employees. That spans founder-led and sponsor-backed businesses as well as established mid- and large-cap players, plus portfolio work for private equity firms. We don't typically work with sub-$50M startups; the search dynamics are different and other firms are better positioned.

How is your firm different from a larger search firm? +

Two things. First, every search is partner-led from intake through onboarding — you'll never be handed off to a junior recruiter learning on your search. Second, we source from passive talent, not from job boards or the same five competitor companies. The people you actually want to hire aren't looking for jobs; they need to be found and engaged personally. That's most of our work.

What does "21 days to shortlist" actually mean? +

On a retained or engaged search, you'll have a calibrated shortlist of 4–6 candidates in your hands within 21 calendar days of signed engagement — each one screened, vetted, and confirmed interested in your role. That's our standard. If a search runs into a tighter talent market and we need a few more days, we tell you on day 14, not day 30.

What happens if the hire doesn't work out? +

Every placement is guaranteed — six months as standard, and up to twelve months on executive and retained searches. If the hire leaves voluntarily or is terminated for cause inside that window, we restart the search at no additional fee. A layoff or restructuring on your side doesn't trigger it — the guarantee is about candidate fit. We honor it, full stop, and we build that risk into how we vet candidates from day one.

Do you handle compensation negotiation? +

Yes. We negotiate the offer on your behalf — base, bonus, equity, signing, relocation, severance. We've done this enough to know what's market, what's negotiable, and where candidates push back. You set the parameters, we run the conversation, and we manage the counter-offer dynamics from the candidate's current employer.

What's your off-limits policy? +

We don't recruit out of clients we've worked with. Standard off-limits is 12 months from final invoice on engaged and contingency searches. On retained relationships and multi-search engagements, off-limits extends to 24 months. Companies that engage us repeatedly get a multi-year off-limits guarantee — the protection scales with the relationship.

Can you work nationally? +

Yes. We're headquartered in Austin but our work is national. Most of our searches are remote or hybrid by design — the candidates we source are wherever the best talent lives. We've placed candidates from Boston to San Diego, and we'll travel for client meetings when it matters.

What's the first step? +

A 20-minute call. We'll talk through the role, the compensation, the realistic candidate pool, and the timeline. If we think we're the right partner for this search, we'll send a proposal and engagement letter within 48 hours. If we're not, we'll tell you on the call — and where appropriate, we'll point you to a firm that is.
